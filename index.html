Tribute to APJ Abul Kalam

Introduction

Dr. Avul Pakir Jainulabdeen Abdul Kalam, affectionately known as Dr. APJ Abdul Kalam, was an eminent Indian scientist and the 11th President of India. Born on October 15, 1931, in Rameswaram, Tamil Nadu, he made significant contributions to India's space and missile programs, earning him the title "Missile Man of India."
